We retain your information:

- **Legal Consent Records:** Indefinitely (for legal defense)
- **Usage Data:** 7 years (for analytics and compliance)
- **Anonymized Data:** Indefinitely (for research and monetization)

After account deletion, we may retain:
- Legal compliance data (consent records, age verification)
- Anonymized or aggregated data
